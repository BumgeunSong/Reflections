    Summary¶

  • The higher class people survived much more than the lower class people. Suprise!
  • Babies (younger than 5) and eldery people (older than 75) have high survival rate.
  • Survival rate of female passenger is envidently higer than male passenger.
  • Male passenger whose age is between twenties and sixties have significantly lower survival rate for baby, women, and elderly people.

  • Only with this data, This means only correlation so we cannot say that Age, Class, and Sex is the causes of death.
